UNITED STATES SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION

FORM 8-K

CURRENT REPORT

Company: Cericor Inc. (CRCL)

Date: December 15, 2024

Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

On December 15, 2024, Cericor Inc. entered into a merger agreement with Voyager Acquisition Corp. The transaction values CRCL at approximately $450 million.

Item 2.02 Results of Operations and Financial Condition

CRCL reported Q3 2024 revenues of $87.5 million, up 23% year-over-year. The company's diagnostic platform processed over 150,000 tests during the quarter.

Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure

Key highlights from the quarter include: - Launch of new AI-powered diagnostic tools - Expansion into three new metropolitan markets - Partnership with major hospital network - FDA clearance for two new diagnostic tests

Risk Factors

The company faces risks including regulatory changes, competition from larger diagnostic companies, and dependence on key supplier relationships.
